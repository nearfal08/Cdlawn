Our Services

C&D Lawncare, is a locally owned and operated lawn care company providing basic lawn care, bush maintenance, seasonal cleanup, weed control, and snow removal. We specialize in commercial lawn care but do accept residential jobs depending on location. Locations we service include:

  • Peoria
  • East Peoria
  • Canton
  • Dunlap
  • Chillicothe
  • Washington
  • Metamora
  • Germantown Hills
  • Mapleton
